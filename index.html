League of Legends

League of Legends is a MOBA (Massive Online Battle Arena). It is a fast paced, competitive online game that combines intense RTS elements (Real Time Strategy) and RPG elements (Role Playing Games) together. This competitive MOBA consists of a arena called summoners rift in which two teams of five versus five fight it out. In these two teams there are five roles of which you can play.

  1. Top Lane
  2. Jungle
  3. Middle Lane
  4. Attack Damage Carry/Bottom Lane
  5. Support/Supports the ADC in the Bottom Lane
